HEROES

126 UNIQUE HEROES

Playable Characters with Distinct Abilities

Heroes are unique characters that players control. Each hero has a distinct design and special abilities. Furthermore, there are over one hundred heroes, giving players countless tactical choices. Additionally, each hero belongs to a specific role and contributes differently during battles.

Every hero has primary attributes that define their strengths. Strength heroes offer high health and durability. Meanwhile, agility heroes stand out for their fast attacks and mobility. Finally, intelligence heroes use powerful magical abilities.

Each hero has around four unique abilities. Some work passively, while others need manual activation. These abilities may deal massive damage, heal allies, control enemies, or offer strategic advantages. Additionally, every hero features an ultimate ability that creates game-changing effects when used effectively.
