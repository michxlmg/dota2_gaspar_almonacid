ITEMS

Items improve your hero’s strength, damage, and health.

Items are a fundamental part of Dota 2 gameplay that allow players to enhance their heroes' abilities and adapt to varied match situations. Additionally, these powerful equipment pieces shape the strategic depth of the game.

600+ ITEMS

Purchased with Gold

Items are valuable objects that players purchase with gold, which is earned by defeating enemies, structures, or creeps. Furthermore, these items improve important attributes like strength, agility, intelligence, attack speed, and regeneration. Moreover, they provide active abilities that players must activate manually, or passive abilities that work automatically.

The strategic selection of items depends on the hero being played, as each hero has distinct needs. Additionally, item choices change based on the current game strategy and situation—whether more defense, mobility, or damage is required to counter enemy threats.
