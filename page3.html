HISTORY

RELEASE

JULY 9, 2013

VALVE CORPORATION

Developer & Publisher

Dota 2 is an official game released on July 9, 2013, by Valve Corporation. It is a standalone sequel to the well-known Defense of the Ancients (DotA), a mod for Warcraft III. Furthermore, this innovative title becomes one of the most popular and competitive esports games worldwide. In addition, Valve spends several years developing it, as the team aims to deliver a refined and professional version of the original mod.

PROGRAMMING

C++

Source 2 Engine

LUA

Game Logic

The game runs on the powerful Source 2 engine, which is mainly written in C++. Moreover, the complex game logic, hero abilities, and user interface use Lua, a lightweight but flexible scripting language. Therefore, this setup offers efficient performance while supporting easy customization and modding for developers.

Revolutionary Gaming AI Milestone

Dota 2 is used to train advanced artificial intelligence systems. OpenAI Five is a revolutionary AI developed by OpenAI that plays Dota 2 at a professional level. Furthermore, this groundbreaking project improves reinforcement learning and decision-making in complex scenarios. Additionally, the AI shows remarkable strategic thinking and teamwork, even defeating top professional teams. Consequently, this represents a major step in the development of advanced gaming AI.
