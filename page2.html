GENERAL DESCRIPTION

COMPETITIVE MOBA

Dota 2 is a popular and competitive video game from the strategic MOBA genre (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena). Furthermore, two teams of five players face each other on a symmetrical battlefield. Each player controls a unique hero with special abilities. The main goal is to destroy the enemy's Ancient, a large structure located in the central part of the base.

This strategic objective requires effective teamwork, precise positioning, and careful map control to win. Therefore, players need to develop advanced skills and master the complex mechanics of the game.
