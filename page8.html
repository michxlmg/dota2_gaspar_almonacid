COMPONENT 5 - GAME OBJECTIVES

Destroy Towers & Barracks to Reach the Ancient

In Dota 2, the main objective is to destroy the enemy's Ancestor, a heavily guarded structure at their base. To achieve this, players must strategically attack and destroy enemy defenses by destroying towers and barracks.

The Ancient is the ultimate objective in Dota 2. It is heavily fortified and requires the destruction of all outer defenses before it can be attacked.

VICTORY PROGRESSION PATH

The path to victory in Dota 2 is sequential and strategic. First, you focus on destroying the tier 1 towers, then move on to the tier 2 and tier 3 towers. Next, you attack the enemy barracks and take down the last two towers guarding the Ancient. Finally, destroying the Ancient guarantees your win. The same process applies to defeat — if your Ancient is destroyed, you lose the game.
