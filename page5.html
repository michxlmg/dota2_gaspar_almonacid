THE MAP

Strategic Battlefield Layout

The Dota 2 map is a symmetrical battlefield with three distinct lanes: top, middle, and bottom. Additionally, it includes dense jungle areas and the fortified bases of each team: Radiant and Dire. Furthermore, the map features crucial strategic points that teams must control to gain an advantage.

Neutral Camps & Resources

The jungle holds valuable neutral creep camps that offer extra resources. Heroes farm these areas to gain additional gold and experience. Additionally, the jungle provides hidden paths for strategic moves and surprise attacks.
